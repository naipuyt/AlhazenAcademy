🌙 Ramadan Prayers

Suhur & Iftar Supplications

Prayer Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ

Bismillah

Meaning: In the name of Allah.

Prayer After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِينَ

Alhamdulillahil ladzi ath'amana wa saqana wa ja'alana minal muslimin

Meaning: All praise is for Allah who has fed us, given us drink, and made us among the Muslims.

Suhur Intention (Niyyah)

وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ

Wa bishoumi ghadin nawaitu min syahri Ramadhan

Meaning: "I intend to fast tomorrow in the month of Ramadan."

*There is no specific supplication prescribed for suhur. The early scholars (Salaf) emphasized making the intention to fast while beginning with the name of Allah.

Iftar Supplication

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ وَابْتَلَّتِ الْعُرُوقُ وَثَبَتَ الأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ

Dzahaba adh-dhama'u wabtallatil ‘uruqu, wa tsabatal ajru insyaa Allah

Meaning: The thirst has gone, the veins are moistened, and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills.
